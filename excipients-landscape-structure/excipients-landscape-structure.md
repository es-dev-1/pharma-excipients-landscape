# Excipients Landscape Structure

*Last updated: 2026-05-30*

**5 routes of administration, 17 functional categories, 121 unique excipients**

Structure: Route of Administration > Functional Category > Excipients > Suppliers

All categories are classified by the excipient's primary functional role in pharmaceutical formulations. Categories and excipients appear under multiple routes when they serve that route's formulation needs.
